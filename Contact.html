Spooky Greetings!

I'm the author of Paralocal. Why the interest in the paranormal? I think being born on October 28th has given me a certain affection towards Halloween and therefor all things spooky. Despite this fascination, I was born a frightful person and will promptly die of a heart attack should I ever see a real ghost, as should be my right. Luckily, A computer screen provides a nice safe buffer with all the thrills of being afraid of my own shadow at 3am when I go to get a glass of water.

I’ve explored most spooky content out there on the web and on t.v, so I was curious to look into any rumours of local ghosts. Doing so, I discovered that ghosts, or at least unexplained phenomenon, are found almost everywhere. Weather or not ghosts are real, I’m not sure, but spooky stories of the darker side of history are always interesting.
