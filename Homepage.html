Do you ever hear a bump in the night and wonder who or what made it?

If you do then you have probably considered the possibility at some time in your life that perhaps ghost exist. Or, if you have decided that they in fact, do not, then maybe you have been brought here for your interest in the morbid horrors of humanity; or, the inherent creepyness of a forgotten building; maybe, the story behind "possessed" relics; perhaps, a curiosity in the ability of the human imagination, and maybe simply a fascination in the unexplained.

Presently, this small website is hosting the macbre stories of the ghost in your own backyard. That is if you are living in southern Ontario. The usual suspects will be found here, ghostly apparitions, unexplained and creepy noises set in all too creepy places. You will also find the true stories that lie as possible sources of these haunting tales and hopefully be reminded of the last time you felt an unexpected chill up your spine.
