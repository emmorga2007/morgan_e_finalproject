Have you ever heard a bump in the night and wondered who or what made it?

If you have, then you probably have a curiosity in the paranormal, abnormal, and downright spooky. Presented here are the local ghostly tales and accompaning experiences of suspected haunted places in and around the London, Ontario region.

A Blur or an Apparition?

Often, proof of ghostly encounters is needed to prove an event. But, when photos are snapped, many are quick to dismiss these encounters. Can we ever truly prove the legitimacy of a photo in the days of computer altered images?
